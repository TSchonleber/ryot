# $RYOT — X Organic Growth Playbook

## Profile setup (one-time)

- **Bio** (160 char): `Husky dad. $RYOT founder. 90% of fees → @animalleague (world's largest no-kill rescue). ryotcoin.vercel.app  ·  CA: CgPu...pump`
- **Header image:** pond photo of Ryot at full bleed
- **Profile pic:** real photo of Ryot (warmer than the anime art for profile use)
- **Website field:** `ryotcoin.vercel.app`
- **Pinned tweet:** the longform post or the launch thread's tweet 1
- **Location:** wherever you actually live (specificity = trust signal)

## Posting cadence

Target **3–5 posts/day, 7 days/week.** Mix per day:

| Type | Per day |
|---|---|
| Photo of Ryot (real life) | 1 |
| Story / pitch / provocation | 1 |
| Reply to a bigger account | 1–2 |
| Quote-tweet of relevant content | 1 |

**Weekly:**
- One new thread on a different angle (not always the launch story)
- One milestone post if you hit one

**Timing:** crypto Twitter peaks 9am–1pm ET and 7–10pm ET. Animal/rescue Twitter peaks evenings. Stagger to hit both.

## Adjacent communities to live in

- **Memecoin / pump.fun:** search `$SOL`, `pump.fun`, `memecoin charity`. Reply where it makes sense.
- **Rescue Twitter:** `no-kill`, `shelter`, `rescue dog`, `husky`, `puppy mill`.
- **@animalleague specifically:** like + RT their posts every day. Don't tag them in spam — make sure your engagement is meaningful. They will eventually notice.
- **Solana ecosystem:** `@solana`, `@pumpdotfun`, large SOL accounts.
- **Husky parody accounts:** there are big ones. Engage genuinely.

## Reply strategy (this is where growth actually happens)

Replies grow followers faster than original posts. Reply to:

1. Anyone asking "what does your coin even do" type questions on memecoin Twitter
2. People posting their rescue dog (lead with the cause, not the coin)
3. Animal welfare news / breeder exposés
4. Crypto-skeptic posts ("memecoins are pointless") — counter with $RYOT as proof

**Reply rule:** never beg. Always give a fact or a story. The buy follows from conviction, not pressure.

## What to amplify

- **@animalleague posts:** quote-tweet with thanks + brief context ("$RYOT funds this org. 90% of every fee.")
- **A holder shares the coin:** RT or quote with appreciation
- **Breeder exposés / euthanasia stories:** quote-tweet with the alternative — "this is exactly why @animalleague exists. $RYOT exists to fund them."

## The cardinal rule

**Never make the post about yourself.** Every post is about Ryot, the cause, NSAL, or the math. The buy follows from conviction, not pressure.

If you write something and read it back as "look what I'm doing," rewrite it before posting.

## Things to avoid

- Buying followers, engagement, RTs
- Fake hype rings / "pump it" begging
- Generic crypto-influencer dialect (`gm`/`wagmi`/`lfg`/`wen`) unless it's actually how you talk
- Hashtags — they read desperate on Twitter and don't help
- Replying `$RYOT` under unrelated viral posts (fastest way to get blocked + ratio'd)
- Daily provocations — once a week max, then they land

## Tracking

Watch these weekly:
- Follower count
- Engagement rate (likes + RTs / impressions) per post type
- Click-through to `ryotcoin.vercel.app` (Vercel Analytics is built in)
- Holder count on pump.fun
- **The actual win:** donations to NSAL

## Long game

Consistency > virality. Three honest posts a day for six months will outperform one viral tweet that doesn't compound. The story is real. The cause is real. The math is real. The site is good. That's the entire bet.
